About "Live Digital Encryption"

P2PAEDIADPE

"Live Digital Encryption"  official Name is:
P2PAEDIADPE -> Pear 2 Pear Algorythmic Educational Difreentual Interchanging Alfernumaric Data Protection Enigma (Don't say it all, just call it L.D.E)


The Live Digital Encryption (L.D.E) is a special type of encryption that concists of two computers having a special type of Certificate. When a transaction is about happen, the sender packes up the information, and "seals" it with it's key. The package is sent to the other computer where, if and only if the reaving computer has that same exact key, it will be opend and the information processed. Thease keys change every day around Midnight PST.
